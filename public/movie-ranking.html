THE BEST MOVIES ACCORDING TO AFN

My Top Four Movies of all-time

Kimi No Nawa (Your Name)

For me this is the best anime movie i've ever watch. The scenes, the plot, the story, the characters, everything is perfect. I highly recommend it even to people that don't really watch/like anime.

Edge Of Tomorrow

I watched this action movie when i was in highschool, probably. Until now, i haven't watched action movies as exiting/good as this movie. For those of you who like watchin action/sci-fi movies, i highly recommend it.

The Greatest Showman

Well, the movie is really simple. The story is also kinda mid, but beside of that i like this movie because it was so touching. This movie really made me cry, laugh, and feel all the emotions that there trying to deliver.

Paranoman

This is the OG stop motion movie. I watched it when i was in middle school and i really liked it. There are a lot of good animation movies out there. But to me, this is my childhood. The movie is about a "freak" boy who can see "the dead".
